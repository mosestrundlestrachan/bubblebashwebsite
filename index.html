Our Mission

The Bubble Bash Guild is dedicated to empowering children with chronic medical conditions by supporting the Stanley Stamm Summer Camp, a Seattle Childrenâ€™s Hospital camp where kids can escape their diagnoses and simply be kids.

At Stanley Stamm, campers enjoy activities like horseback riding, fishing, and exploring the outdoors in a safe and inclusive environment where their complex medical needs are carefully met.

We are proud to ease the burden on families by covering the financial costs of camp and providing the peace of mind that allows parents to entrust their children, often for the very first time. Our team of volunteers, including strong male role models, helps create a nurturing community where kids feel supported, encouraged, and inspired.

Since our founding in 2012, the Bubble Bash Guild has raised more than $270,000 over the past three years through our auctions, earning recognition at both local and international levels. We remain committed to making these life-changing camp experiences possible.

Our Commitment to Service: Beyond fundraising, members of our chapter actively volunteer at the Seattle Children's Hospital playroom on a biweekly basis, bringing joy and companionship to patients.

A Message from the President

The Power of Perspective

"To me, there is no greater privilege than giving back... It was stepping into a place where joy, courage, and community shine brighter than anything else."

This summer I had the privilege of spending a week at Stanley Stamm Summer Camp with three other brothers... Kids with chronic medical conditions are given the chance to do what many of us take for granted. Simple things like riding a horse, reeling in a fish, or running around with friends become life-changing experiences.

Stanley Stamm Summer Camp is more than just a camp. It is a gift. And it is one that I feel honored to have been a small part of.

- Blake Gettmann, Bubble Bash President
